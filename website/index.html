✨ What makes it special

A complete meal for your battery

Just like a perfect biryani, this app is the all-in-one solution for total battery peace of mind. Not just a utility — a joyful experience, packed with personality.

Intelligent Monitoring

Friendly nudges when battery runs low, reminders to unplug at the optimal level, and urgent overcharge warnings — all with customizable thresholds and smart timers.

Automatic Power Management

Auto-switches to Power Saver when the battery gets low, then intelligently restores your preferred plan the moment you start charging. Zero effort.

Engaging Experience

A cast of cute emoji characters delivers every alert, turning a mundane task into a delightful interaction. Modern ttkbootstrap UI with light & dark modes.

Battery Health Dashboard

Go beyond the percentage — compare current full capacity to design capacity, and dig into voltage, temperature, energy rate, manufacturer, and more.

Seamless System Integration

Launches with Windows, lives quietly in your system tray with a dynamic status icon, and offers one-click start/stop of the monitoring service.

Custom Notification Sounds

Pick the sounds that make you smile. Fine-tune how often each battery state reminds you — gentle for low, persistent for overcharge.

💞 Your battery's bodyguards

Meet the Saver Duo

Light and Dark — two sides of the same saver. They match your system theme, watch over your charge, and are never shy about telling you to plug it in.

☀️ Light Saver

Bright, cheerful, and always on time. When your system rocks light mode, he's the one popping up with a grin and a gentle “plug it in!”

🌙 Dark Saver

Cool, collected, visor glowing at 2 AM. He takes the night shift, syncing with your dark theme so late-night coding sessions never kill the battery.

💡 Try the theme toggle in the navbar — the duo follows you around the whole site.

🚀 Up and running in a minute

Install once, smile forever

  1. 1

    Download

    Grab the latest biryani-battery-reminder-vX.X.X.exe from the Releases page.

  2. 2

    Run the setup wizard

    A simple installer gets everything in place. Optionally enable Run on Startup and forget about it.

  3. 3

    Enjoy battery bliss

    The app appears in your system tray and starts watching over your battery right away. Right-click the icon for settings. 🎉

🛡️ Privacy & security

Your data never leaves your laptop

No accounts, no cloud, no analytics. Biryani Battery Reminder is a good old-fashioned desktop app that minds its own business — and your battery's.

  • 100% local — runs entirely on your machine, no internet required
  • No data collection — the app collects nothing, full stop
  • Open source — every line of code is yours to inspect (AGPL-3.0)
  • No ads or tracking — ever
